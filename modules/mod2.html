Ariana Grande is Going on Tour!!

On August 28, 2025, Ariana Grande announced through social media platforms that she will be going on tour in the Summer of 2026.

The singer posted a picture of the tour dates and locations on Instagram with the caption "see you next year." Grande hasn't been on tour since her Sweetener World Tour, which concluded on December 22, 2019.
